    About Us

    This is an online notebook, by authors, for authors. When we created and designed this website, we had the intention to help young budding authors to achieve their dreams of bringing their fictional universe to life. A dream led by many, but supported by few is likely to fail. We here at Castaway Corner wish to help you achieve that dream to the best of your ability. With more than 10,000 users nation-wide, we only hope to grow and develop our website further.
