Your Creation. Your World!

From worldbuilders to authors, from dungeon masters to role players, from creators to designers, Castaway Corner is your free online database to keep track of your story. Map out your story, with each little detail, and bring life to your world. With a free account, you'll have access to the core building blocks of fictional universes (characters, locations, and items), personalized writing prompts set in your world, unlimited document storage and so much more.

Create Roles

From mages to warriors, from dragons to kings, characters are the souls of you world. Keep track of each hero and each soceress.

Create Locations

From magical mountains to forbidden fortresses, keep track of the jewels of your realms.

Create Items

About Us

This is an online notebook, by authors, for authors. When we created and designed this website, we had the intention to help young budding authors to achieve their dreams of bringing their fictional universe to life. A dream led by many, but supported by few is likely to fail. We here at Castaway Corner wish to help you achieve that dream to the best of your ability. With more than 10,000 users nation-wide, we only hope to grow and develop our website further.
